Medical schools

Nutritank has achieved a growing momentum across the UK, from the development of Nutritank societies at over 20 medical schools, to online and social media engagement and the curation of key projects on a local and national scale.

Each Nutritank society “Nutritanksoc” acts as a community hub to raise awareness around the importance of nutrition and lifestyle medicine, be it within medical students’ training , for university student’s in general and even for local communites outside of the university.

If you are a medical student and would like to form your own Nutritanksoc at your medical schools then please get in touch info@nutritank.com and download the blueprint here:
